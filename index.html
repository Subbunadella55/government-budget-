Government Budget Tracker

A DApp to demonstrate government budget tracking using a smart contract and MetaMask.

1. Connect to MetaMask

Click the button to connect your wallet. You must be the contract owner to allocate or record spending.

Not connected.

2. Allocate Budget

Allocate an initial budget to a department. This is a transaction that requires a wallet signature.

3. Record Spending

Record a spending transaction. The amount of ETH sent with this transaction is the amount spent.

4. Check Department Budget

This is a free call and does not require a signature.
